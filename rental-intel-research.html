Swiss-Specific Intelligence

Legal frameworks, hidden costs, and market mechanics unique to Switzerland

Key Insight: Referenzzinssatz Calculator

The Swiss reference interest rate (Referenzzinssatz) is currently 1.25% (as of March 2026). Many existing leases were set at higher rates. A built-in calculator could show tenants they're entitled to up to 6% rent reduction — a feature no competitor offers. This is publicly available data from the BWO (Bundesamt fur Wohnungswesen).

📈 Anfangsmiete Contest

Swiss law allows contesting initial rent within 30 days of move-in if it exceeds the previous tenant's rent by >10% or local median.

  • Flag listings where rent exceeds Kreis median
  • Show estimated contestable amount
  • Link to Schlichtungsbehorde info
LegalHigh Value

💰 True Total Cost

Gap of CHF 300-500/mo between advertised rent and actual cost:

  • Nebenkosten (utilities): ~CHF 150-250
  • Serafe (TV/radio tax): CHF 28/mo
  • Internet: CHF 50-80
  • Building insurance: varies
  • Parking (if needed): CHF 100-250

📅 Seasonal Timing

Swiss rental market has strong seasonality. Best months for apartment hunting: January-February and September-October. Worst: June-August (low supply, high demand from university intake).

💰 Deposit Calculator

Swiss law caps deposits at 3 months rent. For CHF 2,400/mo = CHF 7,200 locked. Show: deposit insurance (Firstcaution/SmartCaution) as alternative at ~5% annual fee.

📈 Kreis Tax Comparison
